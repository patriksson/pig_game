Rules of the Game

Welcome to Pig Game! Here follows a quick introduction to the game.

  • Throw the dice as many times as you want.
  • You can choose to hold at any time and keep your current score.
  • If you hit a 1 = next players turn.
  • If you hit two 6's in a row = your round score AND total score is set to 0.
  • First one to reach 100 points win!
